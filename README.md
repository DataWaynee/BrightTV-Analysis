# BrightTV-Analysis

📺 BrightTV Viewership Analytics Dashboard

A Business Intelligence and Data Analytics project focused on understanding viewer behavior, identifying growth opportunities, and providing strategic recommendations to support BrightTV's subscription growth objectives.

🎯 Project Overview

BrightTV's executive leadership aims to increase its subscriber base and improve customer engagement. This project leverages viewership and subscriber data to uncover actionable insights that can help the Customer Value Management (CVM) team drive growth, improve retention, and optimize content strategy.

The analysis explores:

User demographics
Content consumption trends
Geographic distribution of viewers
Bounce rate and engagement patterns
Growth opportunities across provinces and demographics
Data quality challenges
Strategic recommendations for subscriber acquisition and retention
📊 Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard was developed to answer the following business questions:

1. What are the user and consumption trends on BrightTV?
Who are the most active viewers?
Which provinces contribute the most consumption?
What age groups dominate the platform?
Which channels generate the highest engagement?
2. What factors influence content consumption?
Day of week analysis
Time-of-day viewing patterns
Demographic preferences
Geographic behavior trends
3. What content should be promoted on low-consumption days?
Analysis of weekday vs weekend consumption
Content recommendations to increase engagement
Bounce rate investigation
4. How can BrightTV grow its subscriber base?
Provincial expansion opportunities
Demographic diversification
Personalization strategies
AI-driven recommendations

📉 Bounce Rate Analysis
Overview
Metric	Value
Bounce Rate	59.88%
Bounced Sessions	6,000+
Business Impact

Nearly 6 out of every 10 viewing sessions end without meaningful engagement.

Root Causes Identified
Content Expectation Mismatch

Users often enter sports channels expecting live content but encounter:

Pre-match coverage
Advertisements
Break in transmission
Weak Content Discovery

Lack of personalization may result in users leaving before finding relevant content.

Live Event Dependency

Event-driven channels recorded the highest bounce rates, indicating a strong reliance on live sports programming.

⚠️ Data Quality Findings
Anonymous Subscribers

Approximately 13% of subscribers have incomplete demographic profiles.

Risks
Reduced marketing precision
Limited personalization
Less accurate segmentation
Distorted demographic insights
Recommendation

Implement profile completion incentives and improve onboarding data collection.
